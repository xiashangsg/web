Company Profile

About Us

XIA SHANG TRADING PTE LTD was established in 2009, specializing in high-quality bus parts sourced from China. With years of experience, we have built a strong supply chain network to quickly meet the needs of our customers, ensuring the efficient operation and long-term reliability of their vehicles.

Our Advantages

  • Comprehensive Parts Supply: We maintain a wide range of in-stock parts, ensuring immediate availability for all essential components.
  • Flexible Procurement: We offer customized procurement options for special parts, providing tailored solutions to meet specific needs.
  • Efficient Local Delivery: Our dedicated fleet ensures quick and safe delivery, minimizing downtime for customers.

Our Mission and Vision

Our vision is to become a trusted partner in Singapore's bus parts market. We aim to provide stable product quality and excellent service, ensuring our customers receive the parts they need, when they need them, to keep their vehicles in top condition.

Core Values

  • Customer First: We prioritize customer needs and provide tailored solutions that exceed expectations.
  • Quality Assurance: We adhere to strict quality standards to ensure that all parts meet the highest requirements.
  • Efficient Service: We continuously optimize our supply chain to provide fast and reliable service to all our customers.
